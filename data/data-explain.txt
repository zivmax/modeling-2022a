Data Set Information:

The dataset is about bankruptcy prediction of Polish companies. The data was collected from Emerging Markets Information Service (EMIS, [Web Link]), which is a database containing information on emerging markets around the world. The bankrupt companies were analyzed in the period 2010-2020, while the still operating companies were evaluated from 2017 to 2021.
